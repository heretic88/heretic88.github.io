Who we are


We are a family run business specialising in the Building, Landscaping and Plastering industries and have over 13 years experience working within them. Rest assured that you can expect fast, friendly, efficient and a proffessional experience when we undertake work.


A comment from the company owner Daniel Baker;

"Baker & Son's is more than a name it's our way of life. As a family run company we bring our heart and soul into everything we do. We won't let you down!"

Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is simple. We aim to be industry leaders. We won't stop until Baker & Son's is an household name between friends and families up and down the country for all their trade work needs. We aim to be the largest tradesmen in the West Midlands within the next 12 months and we have you to thank for making it a reality.
